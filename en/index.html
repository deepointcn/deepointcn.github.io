WHO WE ARE

Drive customer's business forward by do things matter

Deepoint Consulting was founded in 2021 as a 100% employee-owned and operated company (EOC). We stand by the belief that our clients and employees are the bedrock of our business, enabling us to deliver excellent services to our clients. Our team is comprised of industry elites, who are specialized in Microsoft D365 ERP and bring with them a collective experience of over fifteen years from internationally recognized companies such as Microsoft, Accenture, and Hitachi Consulting.

Strategically positioned to offer a global reach, we provide cross-time zone, multilingual services (English, Japanese, Chinese), that cater to the diverse needs of our clients. We are an innovative firm with a flat organizational structure that favors agile project management. Communication efficiency is at the heart of our operations. We swiftly respond to project requirements and changes, tackle various challenges, and ensure seamless execution and delivery of every project.

D365 F&O ERP One-Stop Expert Services

Customization

Implementation

Business Intelligence

Consulting

Get insights of D365 ERP, offer best solutions to fit your needs and budget, propose improvements to maximize operational benefits

Support
