Who we are

Drive customers business forward by do things matter

Deepoint Consulting was founded in 2021 as a 100% employee-owned and operated company (EOC). We stand by the belief that our clients and employees are the bedrock of our business, enabling us to deliver excellent services to our clients. Our team, the industry elites, are specialists in Microsoft D365 ERP. They bring with them a collective experience of over fifteen years from internationally recognized companies such as Microsoft, Accenture, and Hitachi Consulting.

Strategically positioned to offer a global reach, we provide cross-time zone, multilingual services (English, Japanese, Chinese), catering to the diverse needs of our clients. We are an innovative firm with a flat organizational structure that favors agile project management. Communication efficiency is at the heart of our operations. We swiftly respond to project requirements and changes, tackle various challenges, and ensure seamless execution and delivery of every project.
