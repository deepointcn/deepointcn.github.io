Our References

Deepoint Consulting, as a Microsoft partner, we specialize in the consultation and implementation of Microsoft business solutions. Our clients span across multiple countries and industries, including manufacturing, high tech, retail, biopharmaceuticals, trading and logistics. Leveraging Microsoft D365 ERP and Azure cloud platform, we offer enterprises a fully functional, one-stop comprehensive IT solution.

Our consulting team boasts over 15 years of experience in the strategic planning and implementation of ERP systems in the manufacturing, biopharmaceutical, retail, high tech, and trade industries. Our consultants work closely with client teams (business, IT, management) at every stage of project implementation, jointly enabling business process transformation and facilitating rapid business growth.

Our successful cases are the best testament to our professional capabilities and service quality. Whether you are a small business or a medium to large enterprise, we can provide you with ERP services that best suit your needs. We hope our success stories can inspire and enlighten you, and we look forward to collaborating with you to achieve success.
