Our References

As a Microsoft partner, Deepoint Consulting specializes in the consultation and implementation of Microsoft business solutions. Our clients span across various countries and industries, including Manufacturing, High tech, Retail, Biopharmaceuticals, Trading and Logistics. Through leveraging Microsoft D365 ERP and the Azure cloud platform, we offer enterprises a fully functional, one-stop comprehensive IT solution.

Our consulting team boasts over 15 years of experience in the strategic planning and implementation of ERP systems across the manufacturing, biopharmaceutical, retail, high tech, and trade industries. Our consultants work closely with client teams (business, IT, management) at every stage of project implementation, jointly enabling business process transformation and facilitating rapid business growth.

Our successful cases are the best testament to our professional capabilities and service quality. Whether you are a small business or a medium to large enterprise, we can provide you with ERP services that best suit your needs. We hope our success stories can inspire and enlighten you, and we look forward to assessing how we can contribute to your team’s future success.
