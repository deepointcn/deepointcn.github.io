Company info

Passion to empower our customers and employees

We established in 2021, Deepoint Consulting is an 100% employee-owned company. We believe that our employees are the core wealth of our company and the cornerstone of our ability to provide excellent service to our clients. We have assembled a team of industry elites specializing in Microsoft ERP, with team members hailing from well-known multinational companies (Microsoft, Accenture, Hitachi Consulting, etc.). Our team has over fifteen years of extensive international project experience.

We offer global coverage with cross-time zone, multilingual services (English, Japanese, Chinese) to meet the needs of clients from various regions. As an innovative company, we maintain a flat organizational structure and embrace agile project management. We prioritize efficient communication in our projects, respond quickly to requirements and changes, face various challenges, and ensure the smooth execution and delivery of each project.
